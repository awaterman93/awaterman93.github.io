About Me...

Hey, I'm Alex! Nice to meet you. I was born and raised in Columbus, Ohio, and growing up, I always had an interest in computers (even if that meant I was mostly breaking them to see how they worked). After earning my Associate of Arts from CSCC, I spent several years working as an EMT and Firefighter. Eventually, I decided to take a leap and turn my lifelong interest in tech into a career by enrolling in the We Can Code IT software development bootcamp. Today, I'm on staff at The Ohio State University working as a Digital Accessibility Coordinator for ASC Tech, while also studying for my BA in Communication. Outside of work and school, I love spending time with my wife and our dog, and I'm highly active in combat sports, having trained in Muay Thai and Jiu-Jitsu for over 15 years.
